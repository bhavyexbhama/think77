Common Questions

Everything you
need to know.

What's your typical project timeline?
+

Most projects run 2–6 weeks depending on scope. A single 3D render or logo can turn around in 3–5 days. Full brand campaigns or website builds typically take 4–6 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline after the initial brief call.

Do you work with international clients?
+

Absolutely. We're based in New Delhi but work with brands across the US, UAE, UK, and Southeast Asia. All communication and delivery is handled digitally, so timezone is rarely an issue.

What software do you use for 3D work?
+

We primarily work in Blender and Cinema 4D for 3D modeling, animation, and rendering. Post-production is handled in After Effects, Premiere, and DaVinci Resolve for color grading. Every tool is chosen for the best output, not familiarity.

How do revisions work?
+

Every project includes 2 rounds of revisions built into the price. We share work-in-progress previews early so you're never surprised. Additional revision rounds are available at a flat rate.

What do you need to get started?
+

Just reach out with a rough idea of what you need. We'll schedule a 30-minute discovery call, discuss the scope, and send you a detailed proposal within 24 hours. No lengthy forms, no waiting.
